Research

My main interests are in the areas of PDE and numerical analysis, with applications in computational electromagnetics, mathematical physiology and mechanics. Specifically, I work on the development and analysis of numerical methods for scattering and spectral problems, including FEM and integral equation methods. Apart from this, I enjoy using mathematics to solve real-world problems wherever they arise.

Editorial & Service Roles

I serve on the series editorial boards of the CMS/CAIMS Book Series in Mathematics, and the SIAM Book Series on Mathematics and Computation. I'm also a category co-moderator for the arXiv.
