Research:

    My main interests are in the areas of PDE and numerical analysis, with applications in mathematical physiology, computational electromagnetics and mechanics. Specifically, I work on the development and analysis of numerical methods for scattering and spectral problems, including FEM and integral equation methods. Apart from this, I enjoy using mathematics to solve real-world problems wherever they arise. In the last several years I've become curious about spectral geometry.
